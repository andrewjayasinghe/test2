The Company

About Us

We are specialists in breeding and growing a wide range of tropical ornamental fish varieties in Sri Lanka. The quality concept of the company is the belief in the proficiency of our workforce to deliver superior quality products meeting international standards. At present, the company exports guppies, mollies, sword tails, angels, barbs & betta to multiple international destinations. The extensive R & D effort of the company which produces new varieties continually is the niche of the company. You are bound to be surprised by new and exciting products, only Nikoi Aqua can supply.

Dushan Thalagala

Group Chairman

Retired commissioned officer in the Sri Lanka Air Force & is a helicopter pilot by profession. Immigrated to Canada in 2001 with his family and obtained his helicopter pilot licensing and related approvals. He is a former employee of many reputed helicopter operators in the world such as CHC Canada, Gulf Helicopters etc. His born business acumen led him to the formation of his incorporated business ‘Thalagala Holdings” in the province of British Columbia in 2013. Business diversification plans of Thalagala Holdings, led to the purchase of major shareholding of the thriving ‘Nikoi Aqua’ ornamental fish export company based in Sri Lanka in 2021 and re positioned all its international operations at the Canadian office located at 6655 Napier Street, Burnaby, BC under the direct supervision of Dushan

Vinoda Jayasinghe

Banker by profession with 25 years’ of work experience at Commercial Bank PLC. A specialist in cost conscious management exploiting the optimal potential of resources. Sanjeeva Benedict De Silva

Director of Operations

Retired commissioned officer in the Sri Lanka Air Force was a fighter controller and an UAV external pilot of Israeli built UAV systems. Therefore, his contribution to Nikoi Aqua, in his capacity as the Director of Operations is one of the strong driving forces to the consolidation of the company’s present position in the ornamental fish industry in the country
