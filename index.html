Sri Lanka is an island nation located in the Indian ocean between latitudes 5⁰ 55’ to 9⁰ 51’ N and longitudes 79⁰ 41’ to 81⁰ 53’ E. It is a nation blessed with Blue Mountains, natural forests, ample rainfall, mild tropical temperatures, wild life, flora and fauna and is a tropical paradise which is home to many species of indigenous aquatic life. As of late, the raising of many varieties of ornamental fish for the domestic hobby market and now, more than ever, for the global export market is emerging as a new business trend. Over 90% of our exports amounts to the live bearer category and Nikoi Aqua is emerging as a reckonable force in the industry which is passionate about the high quality and uniqueness of its product range.
