Beautiful freshwater aquatic life in the Pearl of the Indian Ocean
Sri Lanka is an island nation located in the Indian ocean between latitudes 5⁰ 55’ to
9⁰ 51’ N and longitudes 79⁰ 41’ to 81⁰ 53’ E. A country blessed with Blue Mountains, natural forests, ample rainfall, mild tropical temperatures, wild life, flora and fauna is a tropical paradise which is home for many species of indigenous aquatic life. Since of late, the raising of many varieties of ornamental fish for the domestic hobby market and now, more than ever, for the global export market has been emerged as a new business trend. Over 90% of our exports amounts to live bearer category in which ‘Nikoi Aqua’ is emerging as a reckonable force in the industry which is passionate about the high quality and uniqueness of its product range.
