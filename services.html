Guppies

Hailing from the Poecilia Reticulata family and originating in Trinidad, Guppies have come through generations of evolution. Guppy is an ornamental aquatic variety of livebearer category which is being exported in high volumes from Sri Lanka. Nikoi Aqua, has some of the unique and extremely rare varieties of guppies in our stocks conditioned for the niche export market segment for those that appreciate the quality and uniqueness of their aquariums.
